Description
This movie was produced in 2001. This movie is about a group of 11 people, who plan to rob a Casino in Las Vegas.
Each member of the crew has a crucial role to play, and if that role is messed up, it could cost them the whole plan.
The casino they wanted to rob was one of the hardest casino's to rob in the entire world. Although the risks and dangers,
the crew of 11 succeeded in robbing the casino. There is a series of Ocean movies that were created.
